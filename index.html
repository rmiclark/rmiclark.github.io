I lead Global Planning and Performance management for LinkedIn's Monetization Strategy & Operations team, supporting Sales Solutions xSaaS product line. Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Stanford NLP Group and the Stanford Data Science Institute. I received my Ph.D. from the Machine Learning Department at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Noah Smith.

My research centers on making machine learning more reliable and responsible, and on using machine learning and natural language processing to learn about society from text.
